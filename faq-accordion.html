How do I get started with BlogSpace?

+
Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for a free account, verify your email, and you can start writing immediately. Our intuitive editor makes it simple to create beautiful posts.

Is BlogSpace free to use?

+
Yes, BlogSpace offers a generous free plan that includes basic publishing features. We also offer premium plans with advanced features like custom domains, analytics, and monetization tools.

Can I import my existing blog content?

+
Absolutely! We support importing from most major blogging platforms including WordPress, Medium, and Substack. Our import tool preserves your formatting and metadata.

How does the monetization work?

+
Premium users can monetize their content through our built-in subscription system, tip jar feature, and sponsored content marketplace. We take a small percentage to maintain the platform.

What kind of analytics do you provide?

+
We provide comprehensive analytics including page views, reader engagement, subscriber growth, popular content, and reader demographics. Premium users get access to advanced metrics and export options.

Can I customize the design of my blog?

+
Yes! We offer multiple themes and customization options. Premium users can fully customize their blog's appearance, use custom CSS, and even upload their own themes.

Is there a mobile app available?

+
We have a mobile-optimized web app that works great on all devices. Native iOS and Android apps are currently in development and will be available soon.

How do you handle content moderation?

+
We use a combination of automated systems and human moderators to ensure content quality and safety. We have clear community guidelines and take swift action against violations.
